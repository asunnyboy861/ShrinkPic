## Description

ShrinkPic is the fastest way to free up storage on your iPhone. Compress hundreds of photos in seconds — all on your device, no uploads, no subscriptions.

• BATCH COMPRESSION
Select up to 500+ photos and compress them all at once. No crashes, no limits for Pro users.

• MULTIPLE QUALITY LEVELS
Choose from Low, Medium, High, or Best quality. See estimated file size reduction before you start.

• SMART STORAGE ANALYSIS
See exactly where your storage goes — screenshots, camera photos, and more. Know what to compress first.

• BEFORE & AFTER COMPARISON
Visually compare original and compressed photos side by side. See quality and size difference at a glance.

• DELETE ORIGINALS
Optionally delete original photos after compression to instantly free up space.

• 100% PRIVATE
All processing happens on your device. Your photos never leave your iPhone. No servers, no tracking, no analytics.
