ShrinkPic Support

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe?

Yes! All compression happens on your device. We never upload your photos to any server.

Why does the app need photo library access?

ShrinkPic needs access to read and save photos. We only access photos you explicitly select for compression.

What's the difference between Free and Pro?

Free users can compress up to 5 photos per day. Pro users get unlimited compression, batch processing for 500+ photos, and all future features for a one-time purchase.

Will I lose photo quality?

ShrinkPic offers multiple quality levels. "Best" quality preserves nearly all detail while still reducing file size. "Low" quality achieves maximum compression.

Can I restore my Pro purchase?

Yes! Go to Settings → Restore Purchases. Your Pro status will be restored on any device signed in with the same Apple ID.

What happens when I delete originals?

When enabled, ShrinkPic will delete the original uncompressed photos after successful compression. Make sure to save compressed photos to your library first.
