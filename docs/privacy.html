Privacy Policy for ShrinkPic

Last updated: April 30, 2025

Introduction

ShrinkPic ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Data Collection

ShrinkPic does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All photo compression happens entirely on your device. We have no access to your photos, compression history, or any other personal information.

Photo Library Access

ShrinkPic requests access to your photo library solely for the purpose of compressing photos you select. We do not:

  • Upload your photos to any server
  • Share your photos with third parties
  • Analyze your photos for any purpose
  • Access photos you have not explicitly selected

Analytics

We do not use any analytics services, tracking tools, or advertising SDKs. We have zero visibility into how you use the app.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We only receive a confirmation of purchase — we do not collect or store your payment information.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
